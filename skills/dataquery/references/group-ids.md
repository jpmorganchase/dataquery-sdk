# Common Group IDs - Quick Reference

Share this list with users to help them query directly.

## Government Bonds
- `FI_GO_NOTE_BOND` - US Treasury Notes & Bonds
- `FI_GO_BO_OA` - Canada Government Bonds
- `FI_GO_BO_EA` - Euro Area Government Bonds
- `FI_GO_BO_UK` - UK Gilts
- `FI_GO_BO_JP` - Japanese Government Bonds

## Interest Rate Swaps (Developed Markets)
- `GFI_SWAPS_GLOBAL_CLOSES` - Global IRS Closes (try first for DM)
- `FI_SWP_PLAIN_VANILLA_YIELDS` - USD Plain Vanilla Swaps
- `FI_SW_SF_NA` - North America Swaps
- `FI_SW_SF_EA` - Euro Area Swaps
- `FI_SW_SF_OE` - Other Europe Swaps (GBP/CHF/NOK/SEK/DKK)
- `FI_SW_SF_JP` - Japan Swaps
- `FI_SW_SF_AA` - Asia/Australia Swaps (AUD/NZD)

## Interest Rate Swaps (Emerging Markets)
- `EM_CRV_IRS` - Emerging Markets IRS (MXN, BRL, ZAR, TRY, PLN, CZK, HUF, etc.)

## Swaptions
- `EM_CRV_SV` - Emerging Markets Swaptions

## Credit
- `IN_CR_USD_ABS` - USD Credit ABS Indices
